How to Place an Order

Your orders are always delivered fresh to your door!

Text Message, Instagram Message or Email

Simply send a text to 805-399-0441, email debscookiedesigns@gmail.com, or send instagram message to @debscookiedesigns. I will get back to you as soon as possible.

What to Include in your communication

Cookie Set Name, Number of Sets, Special instructions such as chocolate or vanilla, your name, phone number, address, and required delivery date. I will use your communicated method to get back with you with price and any updates.

How to Pay for Your Order

Once you place your order, please make the first payment through PayPal using debscookiedesigns@gmail.com. The remainder can be paid by cash, personal check or Paypal at time of delivery.
