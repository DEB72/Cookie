How I Got Started in the Cookie Business

  • My Humble Beginnings

    Since I was a little girl, I have always loved baking cookies!

  • Gift Giving

    I've always enjoyed making decorated cookies for every occasion I could think of - baby showers for friends' new babies, Christmas cookies for my husband's co-workers, wedding cookies for newlyweds, etc.!

  • A Cookie Business Is Born!

    My friends insisted that my cookies were good enough to rival even cookies sold at Starbucks, so around February of 2018, I checked into starting a home business. Now I'm the owner of Deb's Cookie Designs!

  • What is my mission?

    In additon to making people happy with my cookies, I want to supply a quality product for everyone. That's why I make my own vanilla flavoring which doesn't contain alcohol. Also the baking powder I use doesn't contain alumimium.
